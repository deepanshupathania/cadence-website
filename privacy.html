Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 8, 2024

Cadence (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through this Privacy Policy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome Extension and website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Text You Submit: Your input text is temporarily sent to our AI partner (OpenAI) for rewriting. We do not store or log your content.
  • Local Usage Data: We use browser storage (localStorage) to manage your rewrite limits (e.g., 5 free rewrites/month).
  • Payment Information: If you upgrade to Cadence Pro, payments are handled securely via a third-party processor (Paddle). We do not store your credit card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To rewrite your content using the selected tone
  • To track your free usage and prevent abuse
  • To communicate essential service updates
  • To process payments (via secure third-party provider)

3. Data Retention

We do not retain your text or rewriting history on our servers. All rewrite sessions are ephemeral and exist only during API processing.

4. Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI: Used for tone rewriting via API.
  • Paddle: Used as our payment provider. Paddle may collect data subject to their own privacy policies.

5. Your Rights

  • You may request deletion of any account-linked data (e.g., billing info)
  • You may uninstall the extension at any time

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Effective Date.”
